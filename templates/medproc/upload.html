Start Report

To get started with building your customised report you will first need to upload an excel file (.xlsx). This file will have 3 columns, the first will be the date column which stores the dates of each appointment. The second will contain the symptoms discovered and recorded in the consultation. The third and last column shall contain the doctors diagnosis. There is no need for headings to be contained in the excel file.

For the interest of patient privacy, there are no features that can take into account specific patient identifies. beyond targeting specific dates, symptoms and diagnosis, the data contained in this result with be general and anonymous. There is a feature on the data page (which you can find from the menu or the home page) that will allow you to purge the current database of records to ensure your data is not stored and kept private.

When you have finished with your current dataset, you may navigate back to this page to upload another dataset, however your old dataset will be lost.
