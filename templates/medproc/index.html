Welcome to MedProc,
your data analysis tool.

MedProc is a tool to be used in the Medical profession, you can upload an excel file containing details appointments, patient symptoms and diagnosis. These reports will allow you to colourfully visualise your data and gain insights into trends and anomalies. MedProc brings the cloud technology to enable medical practises from anywhere and at any size to analyze their data without investing in expensive hardware.
